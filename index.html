Metal

Metal trash mainly includes cans, metal cans, old iron POTS, aluminum foil and so on. These items are usually made of metal materials such as aluminum, iron, and copper, which have extremely high recycling value. Metal recycling can not only reduce the extraction of natural resources, but also reduce energy consumption, because the energy consumption of recovering metals is much lower than that of extracting new metals from ores. For example, recycling one ton of aluminum can save about 95% of energy, while reducing significant CO2 emissions. Therefore, the correct classification of metal waste and placing it in recyclable bins is an important step in achieving resource recycling.

Glass

Glass trash mainly includes wine bottles, beverage bottles, glass cans, broken glass and so on. Glass is a material that can be recycled indefinitely, and the recycled glass can be re-made into new glass products after cleaning and melting, and will not reduce its quality. Compared to the production of new glass, recycling glass can save about 30% energy and reduce dependence on natural resources such as quartz sand. In addition, glass is difficult to degrade in the natural environment, and random disposal will cause pollution to soil and water. Therefore, the separate classification and recycling of glass waste is not only environmentally friendly, but also contributes to the recycling of resources.

Paper

Paper trash mainly includes newspapers, books, cartons, wrapping paper and so on. Paper is made from wood, bamboo and other plant fibers, which can be reprocessed into recycled paper after recycling, reducing the consumption of forest resources. According to statistics, recycling a ton of waste paper can save about 17 trees, 4000 KWH and 3 cubic meters of water. In addition, the recycling of paper can also reduce the amount of waste to landfill and reduce the burden on the environment. It is important to note that paper contaminated with oil or food (such as napkins, pizza boxes) is not suitable for recycling and should be disposed of as other garbage.
